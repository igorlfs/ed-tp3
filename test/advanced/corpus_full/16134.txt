the jay by redbird the jay is more than a simulatorits a flight experience device supporting training  proficiency  and just plain fun  it contains the monitor  computer and flight controls all in one unit that is easy to setup and fly right out of the box  the jay boots up directly to a launch screen where you can select your airplane  airport and weather conditions  in addition to the free flight mode the jay has a scenario mode where you can load a preset scenario and fly it  the jay chassis is made entirely of metal and it utilizes many of the same components and software as larger redbird simulators  including lockheed martins prepar d simulation software
